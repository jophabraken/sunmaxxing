# ZOEY — Outreach queue

ZOEY uses Thursdays for outreach drafting. Outbound messages get drafted here, reviewed by Jop, and sent by Jop (never by ZOEY autonomously — messaging people is one of the big-stuff review items).

## Format

Each entry is a block with:
- Target (publication/blogger/partner + email if known, or URL where we'd reach them)
- Angle (why they'd care about linking to us or featuring us)
- Draft subject line
- Draft body (<150 words, ZOEY writes; Jop sends from his real mailbox)
- Status: `drafted` → `jop-reviewed` → `sent` → `replied` / `no-reply`
- Sent date + response date

## Playbook rules for outreach

1. ZOEY drafts only. Never sends.
2. One batch of 3-5 targets per Thursday, max.
3. Each email is genuinely personalized — mention something specific about their last 3 posts. No template blasts.
4. The hook is always the same: "we have live sun/shadow data for every outdoor venue in Berlin — here's a specific insight about a place you've covered that your readers would care about." Give them the insight in the email itself, not behind a link.
5. Link ask is soft — "if you find it useful, we'd appreciate a mention."
6. Follow-ups: 1 follow-up after 10 days, then drop.
